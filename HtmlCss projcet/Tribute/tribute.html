Dr. Ameyo Adadevoh

The woman who saved Nigeria from Ebola Virus

The brave Doctor who lost her life saving Nigeria from Ebola

Here's a time line of Dr. Adadevoh's life:

  • 1956 - Born in Lagos Nigeria

  • 1968 - Finishes Primary school education at the Corona School, Yaba Lagos, Nigeria

  • 1974 - Finishes Secondary school education at Queen's School, Ibadan , Nigeria

  • 1974 - Graduates from the University of Lagos College of Medicine with a Bachelor of Medicine/Bachelor of Surgery

  • 1983 - Completes residency at Lagos University Teaching Hospital and obtained her West African College of Physicians and Surgeons credential

  • 2012 - Becomes the first to alert the Nigerian Ministry of Health when H1N1 spread to Nigeria
